About Kapees

At Kapees Interior Design Studio, we believe every space has a story to tell — and we are here to bring that story to life with elegance, precision, and soul. With 8 years of expertise and over 250 projects completed for leading builders in Mumbai like Lodha and Ayaam , we blend timeless design principles with innovative solutions to craft interiors that inspire. Our approach is simple yet powerful: • Listen deeply to understand our clients’ vision • Design with purpose so every detail enhances both beauty and function • Deliver with excellence, ensuring quality craftsmanship and on-time execution From luxury residences to commercial spaces, every Kapees project carries our signature balance of style, comfort, and sophistication. We source the finest materials, work with skilled artisans, and incorporate global design trends while keeping each space deeply personal and uniquely yours. At Kapees, we don’t just decorate rooms — we create experiences that reflect who you are and how you live. Luxury interior brand now coming to your city Bangalore

FAQ

1

How long does a project usually take?

Depending on the scope, projects range from 45 days (single-room makeovers) to 5–6 months for full turnkey interiors.

2

Do you take up smaller projects?

Yes. Whether it’s a single room or an entire space, Kapees Interior Studio tailors solutions to your needs.

3

Which locations do you work in?

We execute projects across India, with key operations in major metro cities.

4

Can we use our own vendors?

We recommend using our in-house team for consistent quality, but collaboration is possible on request.

5

What are your pricing models?

We offer design-only, design-build and turnkey packages. Costs vary based on size, materials and complexity.
